This stylish home is freshly renovated to the highest technical and decorative standards. Its cosy and bright lounge opens to a magnificent American kitchen. It has 4 large bedrooms including 3 en suites with showers. Its large rooftop terrace accessible through its spiral staircase offers a panoramic view of Kigali City!

Outdoor, it has a welcoming patio, a flat front garden perfectly safe for kids’ play, a parking, and a large vegetable garden. 

This home is an ideal cosy nest for couples or families!
